# Findings by Theme

## Recommendation Quality (6 records)

**Root cause:** Algorithm fails to understand user preferences, leading to irrelevant recommendations. This causes user frustration.

**User behavior:** Users expect personalized and relevant recommendations, but are instead met with disappointing suggestions.

**Representative quote:** "worst recommendations"

## Repetition Fatigue (1 records)

**Root cause:** Lack of music variety leads to user dissatisfaction. Limited song selection causes repetition.

**User behavior:** Users are listening to the same playlist or radio station repeatedly, causing fatigue.

**Representative quote:** "same songs"

## Discovery Effort (3 records)

**Root cause:** Algorithm prioritizes popularity over discovery, limiting user exploration. This leads to a lack of diverse recommendations.

**User behavior:** Users are seeking new and diverse content, but the platform is not providing adequate suggestions.

**Representative quote:** "can't show me similar"

## Algorithm Trust/Distrust (2 records)

**Root cause:** Users have varying levels of trust in the algorithm's suggestions. This affects their overall experience.

**User behavior:** Users exhibit skepticism or enthusiasm towards the algorithm's recommendations, influencing engagement.

**Representative quote:** "things I think are stupid"
