# Findings by Theme

## Recommendation Quality (6 records)

**Root cause:** The recommendation algorithm is not meeting user expectations, providing limited or irrelevant results.

**User behavior:** Users are expressing frustration and disappointment with the quality of recommendations.

**Representative quote:** "utter garbage"

## Repetition Fatigue (9 records)

**Root cause:** The algorithm repeats songs too frequently, causing user frustration. This leads to a poor user experience.

**User behavior:** Users are expressing dissatisfaction with the music selection, indicating a desire for more varied playlists.

**Representative quote:** "heard at least 10 songs twice"

## Discovery Effort (7 records)

**Root cause:** Users are struggling to find new content, leading to frustration and disappointment. The current discovery process is inadequate.

**User behavior:** Users are having difficulty discovering new music and are seeking better recommendations. They are expressing dissatisfaction with the current experience.

**Representative quote:** "sucks pretty hard"

## Algorithm Trust/Distrust (4 records)

**Root cause:** Algorithmic recommendations are not diverse or personalized enough, leading to user frustration.

**User behavior:** Users are expressing dissatisfaction with the repetitive music suggestions they receive.

**Representative quote:** "same music every time"

## Social/Identity Signaling (3 records)

**Root cause:** Users are expressing enthusiasm and pride through exclusive or trendy references. This signals their social identity and interests.

**User behavior:** Users are showcasing their affiliation with popular culture and trends to signal their identity and status.

**Representative quote:** "bad bunny collecting"
